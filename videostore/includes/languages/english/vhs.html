Video (VHS) TV formats:
PAL / SECAM: 25 frames/sec
NTSC: 29.97 frames/sec
film: 24 frames/sec

Also - despite VCR's being able to play back all formats - make sure that your TV can as well - there are some VCR's that are able to say, play back NTSC video tapes and play them on a PAL TV.
